About Me

I care about the details people feel before they can describe them.

I build websites through the lens of both a designer and a developer, which means I care deeply about structure, clarity, and atmosphere.

I'm a developer with a design eye, which means I care as much about how something works as how it feels. My favorite projects live at the intersection of storytelling, system thinking, and clean execution.

When I'm not building websites, I'm usually refining side projects, collecting visual inspiration, or experimenting with layout and motion ideas that make the web feel more alive.
